What

StorySLAM Oslo is a group of storytellers and instructors who organize workshops, events and storytelling contests. StorySLAM Oslo specializes in communication, dissemination and personal stories.

Ideology

Everyone can tell stories and everyone has a story to tell

What we do

We arrange StorySLAM events in several Norwegian cities. It is a live storytelling contest where eight participants compete to tell the best true and lived story in 8 minutes. No notes are allowed! The story must be true. Based on performance and content, they will be awarded on a scale of 1-9 of three juries: one professional jury in addition to two randomly selected audience jury. The three highest-score narrators then go on to a final round that evening where they will tell a new true and self-taught tale in 3 minutes. The highest score narrator after this round is the winner of the evening!

Why

We want to strengthen people's dissemination ability and spread storytelling by helping people share stories from their own lives.

Services

StorySLAM Oslo offers various services within courses, workshops and entertainment. StorySLAM Oslo's members are both experienced instructors and storytellers and have worked with children, adolescents and adults alike. All instructors are educated in dissemination, storytelling, theater and drama at the master's level. The majority also have educational backgrounds and are educated lecturers.

Grandslam

Finals

Grandslam is StorySLAM Oslo's big final. It was first hosted at Rockefeller in November 2016. On stage, the winners from StorySLAMene stand throughout the year and compete to be the best of the best in front of a crowded hall.

Harstad

Fortellerkonkuranse

StorySLAM Oslo hadde sitt første fortellerkonkurranse i Harstad i september 2018. Arrangementet var i samarbeid med UiT Norges arktiske universitet og Forskningsdagene. I ukene i forkant av arrangementet ble det avholdt flere workshops hvor folk kunne komme og jobbe med sin formidlingsevne og sine historier. Businesses

StorySLAM Oslo can offer different services to businesses.

  • Events:
    Do you want a more unique and exotic form of entertainment for an event? StorySLAM Oslo have storytellers who can provide a varied experience with stories that can give you goosebumps, bring you to tears and have your falling off your chair laughing. All in one evening.

  • Course:
    Do you want to be a better communicator? StorySLAM Oslo can offer tailored courses in dissemination for you and your employees. StorySLAM Oslo has worked with different professions and can adapt to the needs. Whether it's a corporate presentation, an exploratory lecture or a business pitch.

  • Workshops:
    Do you want to combine team building with better communication skills? StorySLAM Oslo can tailor various workshops where you can get to know your employees better while working on personal stories. Join a unique experience and experience getting to know your colleagues in a whole new way. For more information and prices please contact: Post@storyslamoslo.no

School

StorySLAM Oslo can offer different services to schools

  • Events:
    StorySLAM Oslo can offer various events for school at all levels, from primary school to primary school. StorySLAM Oslo can prepare the event based on the wishes and needs. Our narratives work with both folk tales, myths, historical stories and personal stories. The event can also be adapted to the curriculum in different subjects.

  • Workshops:
    StorySLAM Oslo offers storytelling workshops for different age levels and based on the curriculum. The workshops can be formed in cooperation with the teacher and go over one or more days if desired. The majority of the instructors are educated lecturers and have experience in designing programs adapted to school at both primary and secondary school and upper secondary level. For more information and prices please contact: Post@storyslamoslo.no
